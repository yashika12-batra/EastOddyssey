Overview

Leave the busy cityscape behind and head to Meghalaya, the abode of clouds, as you revel in the paradise of waterfalls and caves. Visit Shillong and Cherrapunji, two of the most beautiful places in Meghalaya on this 5-day trip. You also get to roam the length and breadth of Assam that's flooded with greenery! Blessed with the abundant beauty of nature, Shillong is the capital city of Meghalaya that offers plenty to explore for the travellers. Cherrapunji lies high above misty valleys and beautiful rivers, surrounded by heavenly clouds. When here, visit Umiam Lake, Cathedral of Mary Help of Christians, Ward's Lake, Don Bosco Centre for Indigenous Cultures. In Assam, Guwahati's landscape will enthral you all the way! Visit the Kamakhya Temple and enjoy a cruise on the mighty Brahmaputra River! Take this vacation and unwind!

  • Day 1 - Guwahati - Shillong (100 km/3 hours)
     Arrive at Guwahati Airport/railway station and proceed to Shillong. En route, visit the Umiam Lake. Once you reach Shillong, check in at the hotel and enjoy a relaxing overnight stay.
  • Day 2 - Shillong Local Sightseeing
     On this day, eat a healthy breakfast, and visit the Mary Help of Christians Cathedral, Ward's Lake, Don Bosco Museum and Bara Bazar. Head to the Archery Field to witness the great traditional archery game of Meghalaya. Spend the evening at leisure or explore the local market. Stay overnight in Shillong.
  • Day 3 - From Shillong to Cherrapunji (55 km/2 hours)
     After breakfast, visit Elephant Falls and Shillong Peak. Later, drive to Cherrapunji to witness the beauty of Nohkalikai Falls, the tallest plunge waterfall in India. Also, visit Mawsmai Cave, Dainthlen Falls and Krem Suri Falls. You can spend the evening at leisure. Retire for the day and enjoy your night stay in Cherrapunji.
  • Day 4 - Cherrapunji to Guwahati (160 km/4.5 hours)
     After an early breakfast, proceed to Guwahati. Check in at the hotel in Guwahati and freshen up. Visit Kamakhya Temple for Maa darshan. After some local sightseeing, take a boat ride on the mighty Brahmaputra River in the evening. The boat ride will be dependent on the water levels of the river and the availability of the cruise.
  • Day 5 - Departure
     After breakfast, head to the airport or railway station for your back home.
