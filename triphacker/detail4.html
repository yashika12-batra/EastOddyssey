Overview

Arunachal Pradesh means land of the dawn-lit mountains. Its the mesmeric land of the rising sun where the first rays dawn the pretty state of Arunachal Pradesh. It is also known as the Orchid State of India and is host to one of largest Orchidari Almost every district in this beautiful State has its own exclusive and rare variety of orchids. So take a break and head to this enigmatic land for a delightful vacation.

  • Day 1 - Arrival in Guwahati - Nameri National Park - Bhalukpong (240 km/5 hours)
     Upon your arrival, start your journey towards Bhalukpong, which is the entry hub of Arunachal Pradesh & famous for archaeological ruins of King Bhaluka who was the grandfather of King Bana of Mahabharata fame. En-route visit Nameri National Park. Upon arrival, check-in to your hotel & rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Bhalukpong.
  • Day 2 - Bhalukpong - Dirang (150 km/5-6 Hours)
     Post breakfast, proceed to Dirang small hill station in west Kameng district. Its spectacular landscape and beauteous nature will surely please your eyes and comfort your body and soul. We will visit Kalachakra Gompa also refered as Dirang Monastery and perceive the serene land. Witness kiwi orchids, then to Dirang Dzong (fort) which is about 150 years old and have an excellent view of the surrounding. Upon arrival at Dirang, check into the hotel. In the evening you are free to visit the hot water spring, regional sheep farm, Sangti and Yak Research Center. Overnight stay at Dirang.
  • Day 3 - Dirang - Tawang (140 km/6-7 Hours)
     After relishing a wholesome breakfast, proceed for a drive to Tawang and continue to reach the scenic Sela Pass, the only way that connects Tawang district of the state to the rest of the country. Besides its geographical importance Sela Pass is breathtaking place always covered in snow providing some beautiful views of the eastern Himalayan range. En-route visit famous Sela Lake. Upon reaching Tawang, check in to your hotel. Have a pleasant overnight stay at the Hotel/Homestay in Tawang.
  • Day 4 - Tawang - PTSO Lake - Madhuri Lake - Bum La Pass - Tawang (Optional)
     Post breakfast, start your day with breathtaking view of Shonga-tser Lake (Madhuri Lake), surrounded by snow- capped mountains and the valley offer a spectacular view. Next move on to Bum La Pass about 37 kms away from Tawang, it is the indo-china border that lies at 15,200 ft above sea level. On your way back stop by heart lake, PTSO lake, Naghula lake, which will surely make your day. After this return to your hotel for comfortable overnight stay in Tawang. (Mathuri Lake & Bumla Pass tour could be done on additional cost).
  • Day 5 - Tawang Sightseeing
     Today after breakfast head out to visit Tawang Monastery. This famous monastery also known as Gaden Namgyal Lhatse in Tibetan was built during 1680-81 by Merak Lama Lodre Gyatso. It offers a commanding and picturesque view of the Tawang-chu Valley. Tawang Monastery is one of the largest monasteries in Asia. Also visit Urgelling Monastery - the birthplace of Sixth Dalai Lama & Ani Gompa which is run by the female monks. After a late lunch and refreshment visit to the Tawang War Memorial. The evening at leisure. Enjoy overnight stay in Tawang.
  • 6 - Tawang - Bomdila (175 km/ 7-8 Hours)
     Today after breakfast, you will drive to Bomdila. En-route visit Nag Temple & Gontse Garden Rabgye Ling Monastery. On arrival, check-in at the hotel. If time permits visit the popular Craft Center at Bomdila. The Craft Center at Bomdila is run by the female traders. Here you can buy wooden and bamboo products and colorful clothes made by the people of nearby villages. Also, visit the museum to get the essence of the culture and lifestyle of the local people. Return to the hotel and enjoy overnight stay at Bomdila
  • 7 - Bomdila - Guwahati (270 Kms / 9 Hrs.)
     Today after breakfast we will drive to Guwahati for your onward journey. Your arrival at Guwahati Airport/NJP Railway station will mark the end of the trip. Tour concludes with everlasting memories of North East India.
