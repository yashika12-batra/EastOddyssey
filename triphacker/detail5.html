Overview

Take a break from the mundanity of daily life and embark on a mesmeric sojourn to alluring destinations of Darjeeling, Kalimpong, and Gangtok. Darjeeling, a beautiful town in West Bengal, nestles at the foothills of the Himalayas. It offers a plethora of sightseeing places such as Tiger Hill, Ghoom Monastery, and Batasia Loop. Kalimpong, a beautiful hill station in West Bengal, is popular for its scenic surroundings, churches, and monasteries. Just like Darjeeling and Kalimpong, Gangtok to marvels its visitors with its unusual treasures such as Tsomgo Lake, known to reflect different colors based on the changing seasons, and Dro-due Chorten a serene stupa surrounded by 108 prayer wheels.

  • Day 1 - NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport to Darjeeling (About 70 Km / 2.5 hours drive) On arrival at NJP railway station/Bagdogra Airport, transfer to Darjeeling. Check in at the hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. Halt in Darjeeling during night time.
  • Day 2 - Darjeeling (Local Sightseeing)
     Early in the morning, at around 4.00 AM, visit Tiger Hill, a popular place to catch the first glimpse of sunrise and the Kangchenjunga. On your way back, halt at the beautiful Ghoom Monastery and interesting Batasia Loop. After breakfast, head for a half-day city tour that covers Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park (closed on Thursday), Tenzing Rock, Tibetan Refugee Centre (closed on Sunday), tea garden (outer view), Peace Pagoda, Japanese Temple and Darjeeling Ropeway (guest must buy their own ticket). In the afternoon, explore the local shopping markets of Darjeeling. Relax as you spend a peaceful night in Darjeeling.
  • Day 3 - Darjeeling - Kalimpong (50 Km / 02 hours drive) and Kalimpong Sightseeing
     Savour a scrumptious breakfast and transfer to Kalimpong. On arrival, check in at the hotel and rest for a while. Get set for a half-day city tour, which includes Mangal Dham, Deolo Hill, Dr. Graham's Homes, Durpin Dara Hill and the flower nursery. After this, head back to the hotel in Kalimpong for a peaceful night stay.
  • Day 4 - Kalimpong to Gangtok (75 Km / 2.5 hours drive) and Gangtok Sightseeing
     After a tasty breakfast, proceed to Gangtok. On arrival, check in at the hotel. In the afternoon, embark on a half-day city tour and visit interesting spots like Ban Jhakri Waterfall, Namgyal Institute of Tibetology (closed on Sunday), Dro-dul Chorten, Flower Exhibition Centre, etc. Relax during your night stay in Gangtok.
  • Day 5 - Gangtok (Trip to Tsomgo Lake and Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir)
     In the morning, get set for a half-day trip to Tsomgo Lake situated at about 40 kilometres from Gangtok. Perched at an altitude of 12,313 feet, this glacial lake reflects myriad colours based on the seasons. Next, visit Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir. In the afternoon, travel back to the hotel. Relax during your night stay at the hotel in Gangtok.
  • Day 6 - Gangtok - NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport (About 125 Km / 4 hours drive)
     Your delightful tour ends today. In the morning, pack your belongings and get ready to leave the hotel. Have your breakfast and check out from the hotel. Travel to NJP railway station/Bagdogra Airport for your onward journey.
